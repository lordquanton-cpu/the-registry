# The Registry — Glossary

**Classification:** Meta. Canon.  
**Purpose:** Canonical definitions for terms used across Registry documentation.  
**Audience:** Contributors. Curators. Readers. AI agents.

---

## Operating Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|-------------|
| **Discretion** | No client identification retained post-closure. No engagement documentation beyond operational necessity. |
| **Deniability** | Outcomes structured to read as natural consequence. No attribution to client or provider. |
| **Prepayment** | Payment before engagement. Cryptocurrency or structured finance. No fiat trace. |
| **Referral** | Introduction through existing client or verified channel. No unsolicited intake. |
| **Operational handle** | Client identifier used in lieu of legal name. Sufficient for engagement. |
| **Paper trail** | Documented, traceable record. Registry members minimize or eliminate it. |

---

## Payment & Currency

| Term | Definition |
|------|-------------|
| **Structured finance** | Non-fiat payment channels. Trusts, shells, crypto. No direct bank trace. |
| **Escrow** | Multisig or third-party hold. Available for larger engagements. |
| **No refunds** | Standard across Registry. Prepayment non-refundable. |

---

## Engagement Lifecycle

| Term | Definition |
|------|-------------|
| **Intake** | Initial vetting. Referral check. Capacity assessment. |
| **Scope** | Defined boundaries of engagement. Written confirmation for changes. |
| **Closure** | End of engagement. Materials destroyed. No retention. |
| **Milestone** | Checkpoint in multi-phase engagement. Often triggers payment. |

---

## Sector Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|-------------|
| **Flagship** | Primary product offering. Full specification per provider. |
| **À la carte** | Individual components. Purchasable separately from tier packages. |
| **Retainer** | Ongoing capacity. Monthly or annual. Priority access. |
| **Campaign** | Time-bounded engagement. Defined start and end. |
